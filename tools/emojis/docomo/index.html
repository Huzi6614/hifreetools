While Whatsapp may be the father of advanced emojis, the actual heritage of emojis can be traced back to Docomo. In case you want to know the precise date on which Docomo emojis first got launched, it was January 2nd, 1999.

Because people were not used to emojis, the first version looked like binary images with white and black pixels, there were around 176 emotions that used to cover practically a lot of real human emotions.

Even back then, in 1999, people had something to express themselves, although these were black and white. Things changed quickly with Docomo when between 1999 to 2013, Docomo released six wonderful versions of emojis.

People who have been using Docomo for quite a while know how wonderful it has incorporated modernity into its system. In case you are a Docomo user, congratulations because you're actually using something that has initiated the revolution of emoji on the internet world.

Characteristics of a Valid Credit Card Number?

While generating a credit card number for any of the reasons above, you need to ensure that it is valid. The validity of a credit card number is dependent on various factors. The characteristics of a valid credit card number include the following: 

  • Visa Card Number

The major thing to notice in the visa card number is that it always starts with 4. The visa card number comprises 16 digits, and it doesn’t change. These 16 digits on a visa card should appear in a single line, and they have to be uniform in size and spacing.

  • Major Industry Identifier (MII)

MII or Major Industry Identifier is the first 6 digits on a credit card number that gives information about the card-issuing company. The MII discussed below will help you recognize the CC issuer without any intricacies.

  • AMEX: It starts with 34 or 37 and consists of 15 numbers.
  • Diners: This CC card has 15 numbers that usually starts with 300, 301, 302, 303, 36 or 38
  • Discover: It contains 16 numbers that start with 6011.
  • Enroute: Enroute credit card starts with 2014 or 2149 and contains 16 numbers.
  • JCB 15: It starts with 2100 or 1800 and consists of 16 numbers.
  • JCB 16: JCB 16 starts with 3088, 3096, 3112, 3158, 3337 or 3528 and contains 16 numbers.
  • MasterCard: MasterCard contains 16 numbers that start with 51, 52, 53, 54 or 55.
  • Visa: Visa card contains 13 or 16 numbers starting with 4539, 4556, 4916, 4532, 4929, 40240071, 4485, 4716 or 4
  • Voyager: 13 or 16 numbers starting with 8699

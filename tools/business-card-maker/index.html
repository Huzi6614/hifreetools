Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Business Card?

A business card is a card that holds important information regarding a business or an individual. It works as an overview of the company and contains the name of the business, its logo, email, phone number, and address.

What is the Difference Between a Business Card and a Visiting Card?

A business card contains information regarding an entire company, and it represents a whole business. On the other hand, a visiting card doesn’t contain much information about the business; instead, it focuses on an individual, and it is preferably used when you’re looking forward to creating a personal connection.

Do I Need a Business Card for My Start-up Company?

Yes! Start-up companies are in utter need of business cards, as they work as the source of networking. The recently launched businesses need to build a network, as it plays a vital role in building their credibility in the market, and it can be done through the assistance of business cards.

FAQs

How to Make Invitation Card?

For creating your own invitation, select an invitation template of your choice and get started with the customization process. You can customize the background of the selected invitation design and add text to communicate important information with guests.

What Should an Invitation Include?

While designing an invitation card, you need to include several important details according to the event. The basic information that should be included in an invitation involves the purpose of the invitation, name of the honoree, time, date, venue, and RSVP.

Can I Share or Print Out My Invitation?

Yes! You can easily share or print out your invitation card designed with our digital invitation maker after downloading it on your device. Our online service doesn’t impose any restrictions or ask you to follow intricate procedures.

What is the Standard Invitation Size?
